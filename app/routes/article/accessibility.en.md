# Accessibility report

This document describes how the DiVA portal and the DiVA members' individual portals meets the requirements of the Act on the Accessibility of Digital Public Services, any known accessibility problems and how you can report deficiencies to us so that we can remedy them. The DiVA consortium, which is managed by Uppsala University Library, is responsible for the website [https://www.diva-portal.org/](https://www.diva-portal.org/) while the individual members are responsible for their respective portals (https://www.[Domain].diva-portal.org/).

The DiVA-portal and the individual members' portals share the same underlying structure. Each members' portal has certain layout elements, such as colour schemes, and logos that differentiate them.

## How accessible is the website?

This website is compatible with the Act on the Accessibility of Digital Public Services.

## What can you do if you can not use parts of the site?

If you need information from diva-portal.org in any other format, such as accessible PDF, large text, easy-to-read, audio recording or Braille, you can notify us via e-mail: [diva-support@ub.uu.se](mailto:diva-support@ub.uu.se). Each DiVA member is responsible for their own files and content. If you want to share an opinion or report a problem, please contact [diva-support@ub.uu.se](mailto:diva-support@ub.uu.se) and we will forward your e-mail to the correct recipient.

## Report deficiencies in website accessibility

We constantly strive to improve the website's accessibility. If you come across problems that are not described on this page, or if you believe we are not complying with the requirements of the law, please let us know so that we are aware of the problem's existence.

## Supervision

The Agency for Digital Government is responsible for supervision of the Act on the Accessibility of Digital Public Services. If you are not satisfied with our response to your comments, you can contact the Agency for Digital Government and complain.
